About Me

Currently I am a PhD student of School of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, led by professor Xuefeng Yan and professor Mingqiang Wei.

I got my B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation in June 2018 from School of Electrical Information Engineering, Jiangsu University of Technology. Then I got my M.Sc. degree in Control Science and Engineering in Mar 2021 from School of Information Science and Technology, Donghua University. In 2022, I was admitted to pursue Ph.D. degree in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics by passing the entrance examination.


Research Interests

My research interests include 3D detection and tracking.
